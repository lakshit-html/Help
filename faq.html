FAQ

This FAQ page addresses common user inquiries, providing clear and concise responses.

General

What is HelpMe?
HelpMe is a community-driven platform connecting citizens with local authorities to report and resolve issues.

How do I report an issue?
Click the "Create A Deployment" button, fill out the form, and submit.

What types of issues can I report?
Report infrastructure, environmental, or other concerns affecting your community.

Account And Login

Do I need an account to report an issue?
No, it is not necessary to register with an account to report an issue.

Reporting And Resolution

What happens after I report an issue?
Your report is sent to local authorities for review and resolution.

How long does resolution take?
Timeframes vary depending on issue complexity and authority response.

Technical

Is HelpMe mobile-friendly?
Yes, our platform is optimized for all devices.

What browsers are supported?
HelpMe supports modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

Security And Privacy

Is my data secure?
HelpMe prioritizes data encryption and secure storage.

How do you use my information?
Review our Privacy Policy for details.
